Session Format

FutSoul sessions are designed to make it easy to show up and play — no “are there enough players?”, no “who’s bringing a ball?”. Just a smooth, organized run with a street-style vibe.

Format: First to 3 goals or 5 minutes — winners stay on; ties mean both teams rotate off
Teams: 3v3 / 4v4 / 5v5 — teams are made on site
All levels: Everyone welcome
Compete: Play to become the Kings of the Pitch
